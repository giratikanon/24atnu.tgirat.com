About

There’s always something going on at Northwestern University.

24@NU is the first ever collaboration by North by Northwestern and The Daily Northwestern. It’s a multimedia exploration of Northwestern’s people, places and organizations at every time of the day. Our reporters battled freezing weather at 4 a.m., flew in tiny aircrafts at 6 p.m., and even braved chemistry explosions in Tech at 11 a.m.

By telling 24 stories, one for each hour of the Northwestern day, we hope to give you an entertaining, intimate and surprising glimpse into the campus we call home.
