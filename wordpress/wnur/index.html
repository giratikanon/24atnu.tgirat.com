The Host

By Lauren Virnoche

Tim DeMay (Weinberg ’09) and Jens Notstad (McCormick ’09) are the DJs behind Land Squirrel, a freeform show on WNUR 89.3 from 2:30 am to 4 am on Friday night… or Saturday morning depending on your perspective. Not just a music show, DeMay and Notstad branch out with poetry readings and other contests. To hear their show, tune into 89.3 FM on Saturday mornings, or you can listen online at wnur.org.
