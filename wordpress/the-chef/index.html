The Chef

By Paul Takahashi

Mark Coopersmith has worn many hats during his life. He has been a jeweler, a house-painter… even a shepherd herding sheep outside of Jerusalem! But currently, he is the chef at Northwestern’s only kosher station located in Allison Dining Hall. Early mornings from Monday to Friday, Coopersmith cooks up a feast, whipping together spices and flavors into savory soups and stews. Coopersmith says he enjoys the culinary arts and hopes to share it with Northwesterners, bringing both Jews and non-Jews some soul-warming experience during the cold winter season.
